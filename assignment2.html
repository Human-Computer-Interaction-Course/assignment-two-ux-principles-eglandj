Value of Content

Users who keep track of stats manually will need to do some calculations to get more stats based off of the basic stats. Giving the homepage larger buttons that are easy-to-click and where you can easily see the sport without having to read can direct the user where they need to be in one simple click. With this kind of accessibility and usability the end-user will want to come back.

Also it has a "Gatorade" or sporty type feel to the site instead of a plain site that has a a lot of ads and open space and is hard to find what you are looking for. This makes it more appealing and desirable to use for the end-user.
